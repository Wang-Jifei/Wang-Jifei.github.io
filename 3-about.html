Hi there! Welcome to Jifei's blog website.

I am a PhD student at the Department of Geography and Resource Management(GRM) in the Chinese University of Hong Kong.

I studied Remote Sensing and GIS for my bachelor's degree. During my master, I attempted to explore real-world applications relating to critical environmental issues (e.g., air pollution, UHI effects, and forest degradation) using RS & GIS datasets and techniques.

My current research interests include intercity migration in China and population synthesis using big data.
