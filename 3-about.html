Hi there! Welcome to Jifei's blog website.

I have just finished my first year as a Master's student in the department of Geography in National University of Singapore.

I studied Remote Sensing and GIS for my bachelor degree. During my master's study, I attempt to explore real-world applications relating to critical environmental issues (e.g., air pollution, UHI effects, forest degradation etc.) using novel datasets and techniques.

My current research interests focus on managing urban environmental problems based on remote sensing data and GIS analysis
